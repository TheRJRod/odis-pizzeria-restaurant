Our Heritage

The Odi's Story

We didn't just want to open another pizza shop. We wanted to bring the authentic, tactile experience of the wood-fired hearth to our home.

As Corpus Christi’s first locally owned wood-fired brick oven pizzeria, Odi's was born from a passion for the three pillars of great food: Fresh Ingredients, High Heat, and Deep Community Roots.

Every log of oak we burn is sourced from Texas soil, and every pizza that leaves our oven is a testament to the artisan craft we've perfected over a decade.
